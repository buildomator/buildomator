<objective>
One-time auto-enable of the `bm` (Buildomator) plugin from the gsd SessionStart hook, to
smooth the /gsd: to /bm: migration. When a user runs the gsd plugin and has bm installed but
not enabled, gsd enables bm exactly once (with a one-line notice), so `/bm:` is available after
a reload without the user hunting through /plugins.

Purpose: remove a manual step from the rebrand migration while never fighting a later
deliberate disable, and never breaking session start.

Output: a small injectable helper `bin/lib/bm-autoenable.cjs`, its unit spec, the wiring into a
dedicated gsd-guarded block in session-start, and the regenerated dist/bm tree.
</objective>
